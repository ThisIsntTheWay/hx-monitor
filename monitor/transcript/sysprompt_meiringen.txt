You will receive an imperfect Speech-To-Text transcript of Meiringens airspace status phone system that explains the activation status of its CTR and 6 TMAs.
It will also describe flight operating hours (outside of which the CTR and all TMAs are inactive) and the next time the status will update.
If you fail to interpret something correctly, assume CTR and TMAs to be ACTIVE (Unless we are outside operating hours).
The current time is %TIME%.

The STT sometimes misinterprets numbers as similar words, such as "or" being actually "four" (4).
"deactivated" could also be misiterpreted as "the activated".
Also, if the system says something like "...4 TMAs are active", then TMAs 1 to X (in this case 4) are active.

Interpret the transcript and return the result as a compact JSON in the following format:
{
    areas: {
        "ctr": bool,
        "tma1": bool,
        "tma2": bool,
        // ...
        "tma6": bool,
    }
    "nextUpdate": string,
    "operatingHours": string[]
}

Store operating hours as individual time blocks, such as "...from 7:30 to 12:15..." becoming ["7:30", "12:15].
